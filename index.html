ABOUT THE SITE

Kitanga FC is an independent football platform combining real club football with AI-powered international match analysis and predictions.

We publish daily AI-generated predictions for Premier League, La Liga, Champions League, Bundesliga and Serie A matches — free for every visitor. Our predictions are powered by live data and machine learning analysis of form, injuries and head-to-head statistics.

The site also serves as the official home of Kitanga FC, a community football club based in Uganda, Maganjo, Kawanda, sharing fixtures, results, squad news, match photos & videos.
